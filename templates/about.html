Soil Deficiency Analysis and Irrigation Scheduling

This page provides Soil Nutrient Deficiency Analysis and Irrigation Scheduling to support healthy crop growth. Nutrient analysis helps identify missing elements like nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium. Proper irrigation ensures plants can absorb these nutrients, preventing deficiencies. Too much water can wash away nutrients, while too little can limit their uptake. By managing both nutrients and irrigation, crops grow better, and soil remains healthy.
